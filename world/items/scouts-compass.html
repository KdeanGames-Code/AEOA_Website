Scout’s Compass

The Pathfinder’s Verdant Guide

Appearance

The Scout’s Compass is a palm-sized brass disc, its surface etched with curling vines and glowing with a faint green shimmer. Rugged yet precise, it points true in the Living Jungle (Story Ch. 2, Ryn Profile).

Lore

Crafted by the Living Jungle’s scouts, the Scout’s Compass was a tool to navigate its tangled depths. Its green light leads Ryn, guiding Aria through the wild (Living Jungle Profile).

“It’s my eye—finds the way when all’s lost.”

Properties & Effects

The Scout’s Compass reveals hidden paths, its glow marking routes and aiding stealth. Ryn uses it to chart the Living Jungle (Story Ch. 2).

Path Glow

Highlights safe routes within 10 meters.

Vine Sense

Detects traps or ambushes nearby.

Stealth Mark

Boosts stealth briefly when activated.

Origin

Forged in the Living Jungle’s heart by its earliest scouts, the Scout’s Compass was a lifeline through the green maze. Ryn took it in Chapter 2, its glow steady as she joined Aria (Story Ch. 2).

Stats

Weight: 0.3kg

Durability: Moderate

Activation: On use (Passive glow)

Affinity: Nature

Role in the Narrative

The Scout’s Compass steers Ryn through the Living Jungle, its light revealing paths and traps for Aria’s crew. It’s key in jungle navigation (Story Ch. 2-3).

Notable Bearers

  • Ryn: “It’s my guide—keeps me ahead of the tangle.”
  • Jungle Scouts: “Etched it to pierce the wild—unerring.”
  • Umbraeth: “That trinket won’t map my shadow’s reach.”

Legacy

From a scout’s aid to Ryn’s trusted eye, the Scout’s Compass carves paths through chaos to triumph. It rests as Umbraeth falls (Story Ch. 7).
