Camouflaged Cloak

The Scout’s Verdant Veil

Appearance

The Camouflaged Cloak is a 3kg relic of lightweight, leaf-patterned weave, its surface mottled with green and brown hues that shift subtly in light. Stitched with vine threads, it drapes like foliage, a perfect blend for the Living Jungle (Story Ch. 2, Ryn Profile).

Lore

Woven by the scouts of the Living Jungle, the Camouflaged Cloak was designed to meld with the wild, shielding its wearer from prying eyes. Ryn dons it to stalk unseen, aiding Aria’s quest (Living Jungle Profile).

“It’s the jungle’s shadow—keeps me hidden, keeps me alive.”

Properties & Effects

The Camouflaged Cloak (3kg) enhances stealth, its leaf weave granting near-invisibility in foliage. It boosts Ryn’s agility while offering light protection in ambushes (Story Ch. 2).

Leaf Blend

Grants camouflage in natural environments.

Swift Drape

Increases movement speed slightly.

Vine Guard

Light resistance to piercing attacks.

Origin

Crafted in the Living Jungle’s canopy camps, the Camouflaged Cloak was a scout’s tool for survival. Ryn claimed it in Chapter 2, its weave tightening as she joined Aria (Story Ch. 2).

Stats

Weight: 3kg

Durability: Moderate

Mobility: High

Magic Affinity: Nature

Role in the Narrative

The Camouflaged Cloak cloaks Ryn in the Living Jungle, enabling stealth strikes against gruntlings and scouting for Aria’s path. It’s key in jungle ambushes (Story Ch. 2-3).

Notable Wearers

  • Ryn: “It’s my second skin—lets me strike unseen.”
  • Jungle Scouts: “Wove it to vanish in the green—silent steps.”
  • Umbraeth: “That rag won’t hide her from my grasp.”

Legacy

From a scout’s shroud to Ryn’s emblem of cunning, the Camouflaged Cloak weaves stealth into valor. It fades into legend as Umbraeth falls (Story Ch. 7).
