Ice-Forged Steel Armor

The Stormlord’s Frozen Bastion

Appearance

The Ice-Forged Steel Armor is a 10kg relic of blue, frost-etched steel, its surface gleaming with icy veins. Heavy and imposing, it’s built for Wind’s Roost, radiating cold resilience (Story Ch. 6, Zephyr Profile).

Lore

Hammered in Wind’s Roost’s frozen forges, the Ice-Forged Steel Armor was crafted to withstand the storm’s bite. Its icy veins pulse with endurance, now worn by Zephyr to anchor Aria’s fight (Wind’s Roost Profile).

“It’s my ice—holds the storm and breaks the foe.”

Properties & Effects

The Ice-Forged Steel Armor (10kg) offers robust defense, its steel and frost shrugging off blows and chilling attackers. Zephyr uses it to stand firm at Wind’s Roost (Story Ch. 6).

Origin

Forged in Wind’s Roost’s icy heart, the Ice-Forged Steel Armor was a stormlord’s bulwark against chaos. Zephyr donned it in Chapter 6, its frost hardening as he joined Aria (Story Ch. 6).
