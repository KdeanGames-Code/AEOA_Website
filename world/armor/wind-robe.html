Wind-Weaved Robe

The Songweaver’s Breezy Mantle

Appearance

The Wind-Weaved Robe is a 5kg relic of shimmering blue fabric, its surface rippling like caught breezes. Light as air, it’s edged with silver threads that hum faintly, echoing the Temple of Echoes’ winds (Story Ch. 4, Liora Profile).

Lore

Spun by the Temple of Echoes’ acolytes, the Wind-Weaved Robe was crafted to harness the wind’s song. Its silver threads carry ancient melodies, now worn by Liora to aid Aria (Temple of Echoes Profile).

“It sings with the wind—lifts my spirit, guards my step.”

Properties & Effects

The Wind-Weaved Robe (5kg) enhances agility, its airy weave channeling breezes to lighten movement. Liora uses it to dance through the Temple’s trials (Story Ch. 4).

Wind Lift

Boosts agility and jump height slightly.

Echo Veil

Reduces wind-based damage.

Silver Song

Minor boost to sound-based abilities.

Origin

Woven in the Temple of Echoes’ wind-swept halls, the Wind-Weaved Robe was a gift to its songweavers. Liora donned it in Chapter 4, its threads humming as she joined Aria (Story Ch. 4).

Stats

Weight: 5kg

Durability: Moderate

Mobility: High

Magic Affinity: Wind

Role in the Narrative

The Wind-Weaved Robe carries Liora through the Temple of Echoes, its breezes aiding her flute’s calm and Aria’s path. It’s key in wind puzzles (Story Ch. 4-5).

Notable Wearers

  • Liora: “It’s my wind—guides me through the echoes.”
  • Songweavers: “Spun it to sing with the gales—eternal.”
  • Umbraeth: “That flimsy robe won’t whisper my end.”

Legacy

From a songweaver’s cloak to Liora’s airy ally, the Wind-Weaved Robe lifts spirits to victory. Its final notes sound as Umbraeth falls (Story Ch. 7).
