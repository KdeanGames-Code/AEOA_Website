Snowy Feather Cloak

The Skyrunner’s Frosted Wings

Appearance

The Snowy Feather Cloak is a 3kg relic of white, downy feathers, its surface soft yet shimmering with frost. Light and flowing, it catches the wind like wings, crafted in the High Peaks’ chill (Story Ch. 5, Sora Profile).

Lore

Plucked and woven in the High Peaks by sky runners, the Snowy Feather Cloak was made to soar above the frost. Its feathers hum with the mountain’s breath, now worn by Sora to lift Aria’s cause (High Peaks Profile).

“It’s my sky—lets me dance above the storm.”

Properties & Effects

The Snowy Feather Cloak (3kg) boosts agility, its feathers easing falls and chilling foes. Sora uses it to glide through the High Peaks (Story Ch. 5).

Feather Fall

Reduces fall damage and slows descent.

Frost Touch

Chills nearby enemies slightly.

Wind Grace

Enhances dodge speed faintly.

Origin

Sewn atop the High Peaks’ icy cliffs, the Snowy Feather Cloak was a sky runner’s shield against the drop. Sora claimed it in Chapter 5, its feathers fluttering as she joined Aria (Story Ch. 5).

Stats

Weight: 3kg

Durability: Moderate

Mobility: High

Magic Affinity: Wind

Role in the Narrative

The Snowy Feather Cloak carries Sora across the High Peaks, its lightness aiding whip strikes and guiding Aria over chasms. It’s key in aerial feats (Story Ch. 5-6).

Notable Wearers

  • Sora: “It’s my wings—keeps me aloft.”
  • Sky Runners: “Wove it to ride the frost—unbound.”
  • Umbraeth: “Those feathers won’t lift her from my reach.”

Legacy

From a sky runner’s mantle to Sora’s soaring shield, the Snowy Feather Cloak glides through peril to triumph. It settles as Umbraeth falls (Story Ch. 7).
