Rugged Leather Jerkin

The Miner’s Ember-Worn Hide

Appearance

The Rugged Leather Jerkin is a 4kg relic of thick, patched hide, its surface darkened by soot and scored with ember burns. Vine threads stitch its seams, giving it a rough, earthy look forged in the Cave of Flames (Story Ch. 2, Talyn Profile).

Lore

Crafted by miners in the Cave of Flames, the Rugged Leather Jerkin was built to withstand heat and stone. Its patches tell tales of survival, now worn by Talyn to aid Aria’s ascent (Cave of Flames Profile).

“It’s tough as the rock I break—keeps me standing.”

Properties & Effects

The Rugged Leather Jerkin (4kg) offers sturdy protection, its thick hide resisting heat and blunt force. It steadies Talyn in the Cave of Flames’ trials (Story Ch. 2).

Ember Shield

Reduces heat and fire damage.

Stone Skin

Boosts resistance to blunt impacts.

Vine Bind

Minor durability boost from stitching.

Origin

Sewn in the smoky depths of the Cave of Flames, the Rugged Leather Jerkin was a miner’s shield against collapse and fire. Talyn took it up in Chapter 2, its patches holding as she joined Aria (Story Ch. 2).

Stats

Weight: 4kg

Durability: High

Mobility: Moderate

Magic Affinity: Nature

Role in the Narrative

The Rugged Leather Jerkin guards Talyn through the Cave of Flames, its toughness thwarting golems and heat as she clears Aria’s path. It’s vital in mining challenges (Story Ch. 2-3).

Notable Wearers

  • Talyn: “It’s my rock—holds me steady in the fire.”
  • Cave Miners: “Stitched it to defy the flames—unbroken.”
  • Umbraeth: “That patched hide won’t stop my shadow.”

Legacy

From a miner’s garb to Talyn’s badge of grit, the Rugged Leather Jerkin stands as a testament to endurance. It weathers the storm as Umbraeth falls (Story Ch. 7).
