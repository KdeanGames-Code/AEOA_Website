Shadow-Weave Cloak

The Twins’ Midnight Veil

Appearance

The Shadow-Weave Cloak is a 3kg (Kael) or 4kg (Luna) relic of dark, silken fabric, its surface shifting with a faint, smoky glow. Light and fluid, it blends into shadow, a craft of the Tribal Enclave’s silent ones (Story Ch. 3, Kael/Luna Profiles).

Lore

Woven by the Tribal Enclave’s shadow tenders, the Shadow-Weave Cloak was made to cloak its wearer in darkness. Its smoky threads whisper secrets, now split between Kael and Luna to flank for Aria (Tribal Enclave Profile).

“It’s our shroud—hides us till the strike.”

Properties & Effects

The Shadow-Weave Cloak (3kg/4kg) excels in stealth, its dark weave muffling sound and light. Kael and Luna use it to vanish in the Tribal Enclave’s gloom (Story Ch. 3).

Shadow Meld

Enhances stealth in low light.

Smoke Drift

Reduces detection by sound.

Dark Ward

Minor resistance to shadow attacks.

Origin

Spun in the Tribal Enclave’s deepest shadows, the Shadow-Weave Cloak was a twin gift for silent hunters. Kael and Luna claimed their halves in Chapter 3, its glow dimming as they joined Aria (Story Ch. 3).

Stats

Weight: 3kg (Kael) / 4kg (Luna)

Durability: Moderate

Mobility: High

Magic Affinity: Shadow

Role in the Narrative

The Shadow-Weave Cloak cloaks Kael and Luna in the Tribal Enclave, its stealth enabling dagger strikes and scouting for Aria. It’s crucial in shadow ambushes (Story Ch. 3-5).

Notable Wearers

  • Kael: “It’s my edge—keeps me a ghost.”
  • Luna: “It’s my dusk—lets me fade and cut.”
  • Umbraeth: “Those cloaks just mimic my dark—pointless.”

Legacy

From a shadow tender’s craft to Kael and Luna’s twin veils, the Shadow-Weave Cloak dances through danger to victory. It fades as Umbraeth does (Story Ch. 7).
