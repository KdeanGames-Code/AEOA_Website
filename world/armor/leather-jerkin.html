Battle-Worn Leather Jerkin

The Last Guardian’s Scarred Shield

Appearance

The Battle-Worn Leather Jerkin is a 5kg relic of tough, scuffed brown leather, its surface marked by slashes and burns. Gold accents glint faintly along the seams, while azure runes—etched in the Spire of Dawn’s style—glow subtly across the chest, a sign of ancient protection (Story Ch. 1, Aria Profile).

Lore

Crafted in the Spire of Dawn’s armory, the Battle-Worn Leather Jerkin was forged for the Last Guardians to endure the Valley’s trials. Its runes tie it to the Spire’s light, a legacy Aria carries against Umbraeth’s shadow (Spire of Dawn Profile).

“It’s borne every scar I have—still stands strong.”

Properties & Effects

The Battle-Worn Leather Jerkin offers balanced protection (5kg), its leather absorbing blows while the azure runes deflect minor shadow attacks. It keeps Aria agile yet shielded in combat (Story Ch. 1).

Rugged Defense

Reduces physical damage with tough leather.

Rune Ward

Minor resistance to shadow-based attacks.

Battle Grit

Boosts stamina recovery under pressure.

Origin

Forged in the Spire of Dawn’s heyday, the jerkin was worn by guardians facing early shadows. Aria donned it in Chapter 1, its runes flaring as she embraced her destiny (Story Ch. 1).

Stats

Role in the Narrative

The Battle-Worn Leather Jerkin shields Aria through the Spire of Dawn and beyond, its resilience key in battles against Umbraeth’s minions. It marks her as the Last Guardian (Story Ch. 1-4).

Legacy

From a guardian’s garb to Aria’s emblem of resolve, the Battle-Worn Leather Jerkin endures as a symbol of defiance. It stands firm as Umbraeth falls (Story Ch. 7).
