Bone and Hide Armor

The Warrior’s Savage Shell

Appearance

The Bone and Hide Armor is an 8kg relic of cured hide, its surface studded with jagged bone spikes. Rough and blood-stained, it looms heavy and fierce, forged in the Tribal Enclave’s brutal tradition (Story Ch. 3, Dren Profile).

Lore

Assembled by the Tribal Enclave’s warriors, the Bone and Hide Armor was crafted from fallen foes to embody raw strength. Its spikes deter all comers, now worn by Dren to crush for Aria (Tribal Enclave Profile).

“It’s my fury made flesh—breaks anything in my way.”

Properties & Effects

The Bone and Hide Armor (8kg) offers heavy protection, its thick hide and spikes absorbing and reflecting blows. Dren wields it to dominate in the Tribal Enclave (Story Ch. 3).

Bone Bulwark

High resistance to physical damage.

Spike Retort

Reflects minor damage to attackers.

Brute Stand

Boosts stability against knockback.

Origin

Built in the Tribal Enclave’s blood-soaked forges, the Bone and Hide Armor was a warrior’s trophy of conquest. Dren claimed it in Chapter 3, its spikes gleaming as he joined Aria (Story Ch. 3).

Stats

Weight: 8kg

Durability: High

Mobility: Low

Magic Affinity: Blood

Role in the Narrative

The Bone and Hide Armor anchors Dren in the Tribal Enclave, its bulk smashing gruntlings and shielding Aria’s flank. It’s key in brute clashes (Story Ch. 3-4).

Notable Wearers

  • Dren: “It’s my wall—nothing gets through.”
  • Tribal Warriors: “Built it from our kills—unstoppable.”
  • Umbraeth: “That crude shell won’t hold my shadow.”

Legacy

From a warrior’s trophy to Dren’s savage shield, the Bone and Hide Armor stands as a monument to might. It cracks as Umbraeth crumbles (Story Ch. 7).
