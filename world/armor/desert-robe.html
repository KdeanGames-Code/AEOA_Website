Desert Cloth Robe

The Sandweaver’s Dune Shroud

Appearance

The Desert Cloth Robe is a 6kg relic of flowing, sand-hued fabric, its surface adorned with faint dune motifs. Bone shards line its hem, clinking softly, a testament to the Sunken Sands’ harsh grace (Story Ch. 3, Zara Profile).

Lore

Woven by the nomads of the Sunken Sands, the Desert Cloth Robe was made to shield against sun and storm. Its bone accents honor the lost, now worn by Zara to guide Aria through the dunes (Sunken Sands Profile).

“It flows with the sand—keeps the desert’s bite at bay.”

Properties & Effects

The Desert Cloth Robe (6kg) balances protection and breathability, its fabric deflecting heat and sand. Zara uses it to endure the Sunken Sands’ trials (Story Ch. 3).

Dune Veil

Reduces heat and sun damage.

Sand Flow

Eases movement through loose terrain.

Bone Ward

Minor resistance to slashing attacks.

Origin

Crafted amid the Sunken Sands’ endless dunes, the Desert Cloth Robe was a nomad’s lifeline. Zara took it in Chapter 3, its fabric billowing as she joined Aria’s cause (Story Ch. 3).

Stats

Role in the Narrative

The Desert Cloth Robe steadies Zara in the Sunken Sands, its resilience thwarting storms and guiding Aria through shifting dunes. It’s key in desert navigation (Story Ch. 3-4).

Legacy

From a nomad’s mantle to Zara’s mark of endurance, the Desert Cloth Robe flows through hardship to triumph. It settles as Umbraeth’s shadow fades (Story Ch. 7).
