Woven Reed Robe

The Healer’s Verdant Grace

Appearance

The Woven Reed Robe is a 4kg relic of green, plaited reeds, its surface rustling faintly with each move. Soft and flexible, it’s adorned with tiny flower buds, a living weave from the Tribal Enclave (Story Ch. 3, Mirra Profile).

Lore

Crafted by the healers of the Tribal Enclave, the Woven Reed Robe was made to mend and protect. Its reeds pulse with life, now worn by Mirra to soothe Aria’s band (Tribal Enclave Profile).

“It’s the earth’s breath—mends us as we fight.”

Properties & Effects

The Woven Reed Robe (4kg) offers light protection, its living reeds aiding recovery. Mirra uses it to heal in the Tribal Enclave’s battles (Story Ch. 3).

Reed Mend

Boosts minor healing over time.

Green Veil

Reduces nature-based damage slightly.

Flower Pulse

Enhances nearby allies’ stamina faintly.

Origin

Plaited in the Tribal Enclave’s reed groves, the Woven Reed Robe was a healer’s gift to the wounded. Mirra took it in Chapter 3, its buds blooming as she joined Aria (Story Ch. 3).

Stats

Weight: 4kg

Durability: Moderate

Mobility: High

Magic Affinity: Nature

Role in the Narrative

The Woven Reed Robe sustains Mirra in the Tribal Enclave, its healing aiding Aria’s crew against gruntlings. It’s vital in group recovery (Story Ch. 3-4).

Notable Wearers

  • Mirra: “It’s my balm—keeps us whole.”
  • Tribal Healers: “Wove it to heal the broken—evergreen.”
  • Umbraeth: “That frail weave won’t mend her fate.”

Legacy

From a healer’s robe to Mirra’s nurturing shroud, the Woven Reed Robe restores hope through strife. It blossoms as Umbraeth falls (Story Ch. 7).
