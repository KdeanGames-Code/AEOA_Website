Background

Ryn is a seasoned scavenger of the Valley of the Ancients, forged by years dodging fangs, thorns, and Umbraeth’s curse in the Living Jungle’s depths. Born amidst the kapok trees and crimson orchids, he’s survived raptor dives and gruntling raids, his gray fur scarred by the Valley’s rot. Meeting Aria at the Spire of Dawn, he hears the wind scream when she claims the Heartstone, recognizing her Elyra’s fire blazing in her wild hair.
