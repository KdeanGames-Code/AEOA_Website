Gauntlet of Storms

The Stormlord’s Thunder Fist

Appearance

The Gauntlet of Storms is a 0.7m relic of forged steel, its surface glowing with a vibrant blue. Etched with swirling storm patterns, it crackles faintly with wind energy, its plates rugged yet regal from Wind’s Roost (Story Ch. 6, Zephyr Profile).

Lore

Forged atop Wind’s Roost by stormcallers, the Gauntlet of Storms was imbued with the mountain’s tempest to command the skies. Its blue glow roars with power, now wielded by Zephyr to bolster Aria against Umbraeth (Wind’s Roost Profile).

“It bends the storm to my will—Umbraeth will break.”

Powers & Effects

The Gauntlet of Storms summons winds at 7.9 m/s, its blue glow unleashing gusts to scatter foes and empower strikes. Zephyr uses it to dominate battles in the peaks (Story Ch. 6).

Storm Fist

Wind-enhanced strikes at 7.9 m/s.

Tempest Blast

Wide gust knocks back enemies.

Thunder Charge

Brief wind burst boosts movement speed.

Origin

Crafted in Wind’s Roost’s icy forges, the Gauntlet of Storms was a gift to the stormlords, its power drawn from endless gales. Zephyr claimed it in Chapter 6, its glow surging as he joined Aria (Story Ch. 6).

Stats

Length: 0.7m

Weight: 2.8kg

Wind Speed: 7.9 m/s

Magic Affinity: Wind

Role in the Narrative

The Gauntlet of Storms anchors Zephyr’s might at Wind’s Roost, its winds blasting Umbraeth’s forces and aiding Aria’s final push. It’s crucial in storm-swept battles (Story Ch. 6-7).

Notable Wielders

  • Zephyr: “It’s my storm—keeps the skies ours.”
  • Stormlords: “Forged it to rule the winds—unyielding.”
  • Umbraeth: “That gauntlet’s breeze won’t shift my shadow.”

Legacy

From a stormlord’s fist to Zephyr’s thunderous ally, the Gauntlet of Storms rages through peril to triumph. Its final gusts in the Abyssal Rift herald Umbraeth’s collapse (Story Ch. 7).
