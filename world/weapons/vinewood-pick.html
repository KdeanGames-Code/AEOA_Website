Vinewood Pick

The Flamecarver’s Verdant Edge

Appearance

The Vinewood Pick is a 1m staff of gnarled vinewood, its surface alive with a faint green glow. Twisted vines form its haft, culminating in a sharp, curved steel tip weathered by ash and flame. Its organic design pulses subtly, a relic of the Cave of Flames’ harsh beauty (Story Ch. 2, Talyn Profile).

Lore

Crafted from the living roots of the Cave of Flames, the Vinewood Pick was forged by early survivors to break through stone and fend off threats. Its green glow whispers of the jungle’s resilience, now wielded by Talyn to carve a path alongside Aria (Cave of Flames Profile).

“It’s more than a tool—it’s the jungle’s will in my hands.”

Powers & Effects

The Vinewood Pick strikes at 7.3 m/s, its green glow channeling minor environmental magic to shatter obstacles or stun foes. Its living essence aids Talyn in navigating treacherous terrain (Story Ch. 2).

Origin

Born in the depths of the Cave of Flames, the Vinewood Pick was shaped by survivors blending jungle vines with forged steel. Talyn claimed it in Chapter 2, its glow a lifeline amidst ash and ruin (Story Ch. 2).

Stats

Attack Speed: 7.3 m/s

Magic Affinity: Nature

Role in the Narrative

The Vinewood Pick anchors Talyn’s survival, breaking through the Cave of Flames and aiding Aria against gruntlings and obstacles. Its utility shapes their journey through the jungle’s heart (Story Ch. 2-3).

Notable Wielders

Legacy

From a survivor’s tool to Talyn’s emblem of grit, the Vinewood Pick carves a tale of endurance. Its final strikes in the Abyssal Rift echo the jungle’s defiance (Story Ch. 7).
