Shadow Dagger

The Shadowstalker’s Silent Fang

Appearance

The Shadow Dagger is a 0.5m relic of dark steel, its blade cloaked in a faint black glow that seems to swallow light. Its hilt, wrapped in shadow-weave cloth, is sleek and unadorned, designed for stealth in the Tribal Enclave’s night (Story Ch. 3, Kael Profile).

Lore

Forged in the Tribal Enclave’s shadowed forges, the Shadow Dagger was a tool of rivals and assassins, its black glow tied to ancient rites of betrayal. Kael wields it now, a blade of cunning against Umbraeth’s forces (Tribal Enclave Profile).

“It slips where light fails—silent and sure.”

Powers & Effects

The Shadow Dagger strikes at 8.5 m/s, its black glow enhancing stealth and precision. Kael uses it to pierce gruntlings and rivals with deadly efficiency (Story Ch. 3).

Shadow Strike

Swift cuts at 8.5 m/s from the dark.

Veil Pierce

Increased damage to unaware targets.

Gloom Flicker

Brief invisibility burst for repositioning.

Origin

Crafted in the Tribal Enclave’s underbelly, the Shadow Dagger was born from a pact with shadow spirits, its edge honed for treachery. Kael seized it in Chapter 3, turning it toward Aria’s cause (Story Ch. 3).

Stats

Length: 0.5m

Weight: 0.7kg

Attack Speed: 8.5 m/s

Magic Affinity: Shadow

Role in the Narrative

The Shadow Dagger aids Kael in outmaneuvering foes in the Tribal Enclave, its stealth strikes thinning Umbraeth’s ranks. It complements Aria’s light, crucial in rival showdowns (Story Ch. 3-4).

Notable Wielders

  • Kael: “It’s my edge in the dark—cuts deeper than they see.”
  • Enclave Assassins: “Forged it for silence—shadow’s own.”
  • Umbraeth: “That little blade won’t find my heart.”

Legacy

From an assassin’s tool to Kael’s mark of guile, the Shadow Dagger weaves a tale of stealth and redemption. Its final thrust in the Abyssal Rift shadows Umbraeth’s fall (Story Ch. 7).
