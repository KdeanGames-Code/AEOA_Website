Bone Axe

The Bonebreaker’s Crimson Wrath

Appearance

The Bone Axe is a 1.4m relic of brutal design, its haft carved from dark wood and wrapped in sinew, topped with a jagged bone blade that glows a fierce red. Scratches and bloodstains mar its surface, radiating raw power from the Tribal Enclave (Story Ch. 3, Dren Profile).

Lore

Forged in the Tribal Enclave from the bones of a fallen beast, the Bone Axe was a warrior’s badge, steeped in rites of strength. Its red glow pulses with primal fury, now swung by Dren to reclaim his honor alongside Aria (Tribal Enclave Profile).

“It’s my rage made real—Umbraeth will bleed.”

Powers & Effects

The Bone Axe cleaves at 8.2 m/s, its red glow amplifying brute force to shatter armor and bone. Dren wields it to crush Umbraeth’s gruntlings with relentless strikes (Story Ch. 3).

Crimson Cleave

Heavy swings at 8.2 m/s break defenses.

Bone Shatter

Enhanced impact crushes armor.

Fury Surge

Brief burst of speed for consecutive hits.

Origin

Crafted in the Tribal Enclave’s firepits, the Bone Axe was born from a beast slain in ritual combat. Dren took it up in Chapter 3, its glow flaring as he swore to fight with Aria (Story Ch. 3).

Stats

Length: 1.4m

Weight: 3.5kg

Attack Speed: 8.2 m/s

Magic Affinity: Blood

Role in the Narrative

The Bone Axe fuels Dren’s redemption, smashing through Umbraeth’s forces in the Tribal Enclave and beyond. Its raw power bolsters Aria’s fight, pivotal in clashes with Kael and gruntlings (Story Ch. 3-4).

Notable Wielders

  • Dren: “It’s my fury—my proof I’m no coward.”
  • Tribal Warriors: “Forged it from bone—strength eternal.”
  • Umbraeth: “That crude axe won’t carve my end.”

Legacy

From a warrior’s mark to Dren’s tool of vengeance, the Bone Axe carves a path of fury and honor. Its final blows in the Abyssal Rift seal a legacy of defiance (Story Ch. 7).
