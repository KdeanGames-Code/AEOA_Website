Wind Song

The Songweaver’s Gentle Breeze

Appearance

The Wind Song is a shimmering blue melody, its airy notes swirling in a soft, flowing spiral. Gentle and ethereal, it hums through the Temple of Echoes (Story Ch. 4, Liora Profile).

Lore

Sung by the Temple of Echoes’ acolytes, the Wind Song was a hymn to calm the storm’s rage. Its breeze lifts Liora’s flute, soothing Aria’s band (Temple of Echoes Profile).

“It’s my lullaby—eases the wind, steadies the heart.”

Properties & Effects

The Wind Song weaves a calming breeze, its melody restoring focus and easing strain. Liora uses it to mend spirits in the Temple (Story Ch. 4).

Calm Note

Restores minor stamina to allies within 6 meters.

Echo Lift

Reduces stress effects for 10 seconds.

Wind Grace

Boosts ally movement speed faintly.

Origin

Composed in the Temple of Echoes’ wind-swept halls, the Wind Song was a songweaver’s balm for the weary. Liora learned it in Chapter 4, its notes rising as she joined Aria (Story Ch. 4).

Stats

Range: 6 meters

Duration: 10 seconds

Cooldown: 25 seconds

Magic Affinity: Wind

Role in the Narrative

The Wind Song steadies Liora’s allies in the Temple of Echoes, its breeze easing their fight and aiding Aria’s resolve. It’s key in wind puzzles and recovery (Story Ch. 4-5).

Notable Wielders

  • Liora: “It’s my tune—lifts us when we falter.”
  • Songweavers: “Hummed it to tame the gales—serene.”
  • Umbraeth: “That breeze won’t hush my shadow’s roar.”

Legacy

From a songweaver’s chant to Liora’s soothing ally, the Wind Song carries peace through strife. It fades gently as Umbraeth falls (Story Ch. 7).
