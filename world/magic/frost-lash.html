Frost Lash

The Skyrunner’s Icy Whip

Appearance

The Frost Lash is a whip of pale blue ice, its crystalline tendrils crackling with frost as they lash out. Sharp and cold, it gleams in the High Peaks’ chill (Story Ch. 5, Sora Profile).

Lore

Forged by the High Peaks’ sky runners, the Frost Lash was a tool to tame the icy winds. Its chill binds Sora’s foes, aiding Aria’s ascent (High Peaks Profile).

“It’s my sting—freezes them where they stand.”

Properties & Effects

The Frost Lash strikes with icy precision, its whip freezing enemies and slowing their advance. Sora wields it to control the High Peaks’ battles (Story Ch. 5).

Ice Whip

Deals cold damage up to 6 meters away.

Frost Bind

Slows target movement by 30% for 4 seconds.

Chill Snap

Small chance to freeze target for 1 second.

Origin

Shaped in the High Peaks’ frozen heights, the Frost Lash was a sky runner’s lash against the storm. Sora claimed it in Chapter 5, its ice cracking as she joined Aria (Story Ch. 5).

Stats

Range: 6 meters

Duration: Instant (Slow: 4s)

Cooldown: 14 seconds

Magic Affinity: Wind

Role in the Narrative

The Frost Lash binds foes in the High Peaks, its ice slowing threats and aiding Sora’s whip strikes for Aria. It’s key in mountain skirmishes (Story Ch. 5-6).

Notable Wielders

  • Sora: “It’s my lash—pins them cold.”
  • Sky Runners: “Wove it to whip the frost—unyielding.”
  • Umbraeth: “That ice won’t hold my shadow still.”

Legacy

From a sky runner’s tool to Sora’s frigid strike, the Frost Lash chills danger to victory. It melts as Umbraeth falls (Story Ch. 7).
