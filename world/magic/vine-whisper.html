Vine Whisper

The Scout’s Verdant Snare

Appearance

The Vine Whisper is a coil of green, living tendrils, its shimmering strands rustling as they emerge from the ground or air. Subtle and swift, it strikes from the Living Jungle’s embrace (Story Ch. 2, Ryn Profile).

Lore

Grown from the Living Jungle’s ancient roots, the Vine Whisper was a scout’s tool to trap and protect. Its tendrils answer Ryn’s call, binding her foes to aid Aria (Living Jungle Profile).

“It’s the jungle’s grip—holds them while I fade.”

Properties & Effects

The Vine Whisper summons tendrils to ensnare, its green coils rooting enemies and aiding stealth. Ryn wields it to control the battlefield (Story Ch. 2).

Root Snare

Immobilizes foes within 3 meters for 5 seconds.

Leaf Shroud

Boosts caster stealth briefly post-cast.

Vine Lash

Deals light damage to snared targets.

Origin

Whispered into being by the Living Jungle’s scouts, the Vine Whisper was a gift of the wild to thwart invaders. Ryn honed it in Chapter 2, its coils tightening as she joined Aria (Story Ch. 2).

Stats

Range: 3 meters

Duration: 5 seconds

Cooldown: 15 seconds

Magic Affinity: Nature

Role in the Narrative

The Vine Whisper traps gruntlings in the Living Jungle, its tendrils giving Ryn the edge to scout and strike for Aria. It’s key in stealth ambushes (Story Ch. 2-3).

Notable Wielders

  • Ryn: “It’s my snare—keeps them still while I move.”
  • Jungle Scouts: “Grew it to guard our paths—silent strength.”
  • Umbraeth: “Those vines won’t root my shadow.”

Legacy

From a scout’s trap to Ryn’s cunning bind, the Vine Whisper coils through danger to victory. It rests as Umbraeth falls (Story Ch. 7).
