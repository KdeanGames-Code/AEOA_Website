Ember Pulse

The Miner’s Fiery Surge

Appearance

The Ember Pulse is a burst of orange-red flame, its flickering embers swirling in a tight, radiant arc. Born in the Cave of Flames, it crackles with heat and grit (Story Ch. 2, Talyn Profile).

Lore

Forged in the Cave of Flames’ molten depths, the Ember Pulse was a miner’s spark to fend off the dark. Its fire fuels Talyn’s resolve, burning bright for Aria (Cave of Flames Profile).

“It’s my blaze—turns rock to ash and foes to dust.”

Properties & Effects

The Ember Pulse unleashes a fiery wave, its heat scorching close enemies and lingering briefly. Talyn wields it to clear the Cave’s threats (Story Ch. 2).

Flame Wave

Deals fire damage within 2 meters.

Ember Burn

Burns targets for 3 seconds post-hit.

Heat Surge

Boosts caster’s next attack slightly.

Origin

Kindled in the Cave of Flames by miners facing collapse, the Ember Pulse was a desperate flare of survival. Talyn claimed it in Chapter 2, its flames roaring as she joined Aria (Story Ch. 2).

Stats

Range: 2 meters

Duration: Instant (Burn: 3s)

Cooldown: 10 seconds

Magic Affinity: Fire

Role in the Narrative

The Ember Pulse blasts golems in the Cave of Flames, its fire giving Talyn the power to break through for Aria. It’s key in close-quarters fights (Story Ch. 2-3).

Notable Wielders

  • Talyn: “It’s my spark—burns through anything.”
  • Cave Miners: “Lit it to fend off the dark—unquenched.”
  • Umbraeth: “That flicker won’t singe my shadow.”

Legacy

From a miner’s flare to Talyn’s fiery might, the Ember Pulse sears through peril to triumph. It cools as Umbraeth falls (Story Ch. 7).
