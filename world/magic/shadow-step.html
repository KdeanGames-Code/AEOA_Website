Shadow Step

The Twins’ Dark Flicker

Appearance

The Shadow Step is a flicker of dark mist, its smoky tendrils swirling briefly before vanishing. Silent and swift, it dances through the Tribal Enclave’s gloom (Story Ch. 3, Kael/Luna Profiles).

Lore

Crafted by the Tribal Enclave’s shadow tenders, the Shadow Step was a hunter’s trick to slip through danger. Its mist cloaks Kael and Luna, striking for Aria (Tribal Enclave Profile).

“It’s our blink—gone before they see us.”

Properties & Effects

The Shadow Step teleports the caster a short distance, its dark mist enhancing evasion and positioning. Kael and Luna wield it to flank in the Tribal Enclave (Story Ch. 3).

Dark Shift

Teleports caster up to 5 meters instantly.

Mist Fade

Grants brief stealth post-teleport.

Shadow Strike

Boosts next attack damage slightly.

Origin

Woven in the Tribal Enclave’s deepest shadows, the Shadow Step was a twin gift for silent stalkers. Kael and Luna mastered it in Chapter 3, its mist parting as they joined Aria (Story Ch. 3).

Stats

Range: 5 meters (teleport)

Duration: Instant (Stealth: 3s)

Cooldown: 12 seconds

Magic Affinity: Shadow

Role in the Narrative

The Shadow Step lets Kael and Luna vanish in the Tribal Enclave, its teleport aiding dagger strikes and scouting for Aria. It’s key in shadow ambushes (Story Ch. 3-5).

Notable Wielders

  • Kael: “It’s my skip—keeps me untouchable.”
  • Luna: “It’s my shade—lets me slip and stab.”
  • Umbraeth: “That flicker’s just a shadow of my own.”

Legacy

From a shadow tender’s art to Kael and Luna’s twin dance, the Shadow Step glides through peril to triumph. It fades as Umbraeth does (Story Ch. 7).
