Storm Call

The Stormlord’s Thunderous Wrath

Appearance

The Storm Call is a swirling blue tempest, its winds crackling with lightning and roaring with thunder. Bold and fierce, it rages atop Wind’s Roost (Story Ch. 6, Zephyr Profile).

Lore

Summoned by Wind’s Roost’s stormlords, the Storm Call was a force to command the skies. Its thunder empowers Zephyr, blasting Umbraeth’s shadow for Aria (Wind’s Roost Profile).

“It’s my storm—sweeps them away like dust.”

Properties & Effects

The Storm Call unleashes a wide tempest, its winds and lightning scattering foes with raw power. Zephyr wields it to dominate at Wind’s Roost (Story Ch. 6).

Tempest Blast

Deals wind damage within 8 meters.

Thunder Strike

Small chance to stun foes for 1 second.

Gale Push

Knocks back enemies lightly.

Origin

Called forth in Wind’s Roost’s icy heights, the Storm Call was a stormlord’s roar against the void. Zephyr harnessed it in Chapter 6, its winds howling as he joined Aria (Story Ch. 6).

Stats

Range: 8 meters

Duration: Instant (Stun: 1s)

Cooldown: 18 seconds

Magic Affinity: Wind

Role in the Narrative

The Storm Call thunders through Wind’s Roost, its tempest scattering Umbraeth’s forces and bolstering Aria’s final stand. It’s key in storm-swept battles (Story Ch. 6-7).

Notable Wielders

  • Zephyr: “It’s my gale—blows them off the peak.”
  • Stormlords: “Rang it to rule the skies—unstoppable.”
  • Umbraeth: “That wind won’t shift my shadow’s weight.”

Legacy

From a stormlord’s cry to Zephyr’s mighty blast, the Storm Call rages through chaos to triumph. It calms as Umbraeth falls (Story Ch. 7).
