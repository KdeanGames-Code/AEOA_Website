Light Rune

The Guardian’s Radiant Mark

Appearance

The Light Rune is an azure glyph, its shimmering lines pulsing with a soft, radiant glow. Etched in the air or on stone, it flares bright at the Spire of Dawn, a beacon of hope (Story Ch. 1, Aria Profile).

Lore

Born in the Spire of Dawn’s ancient halls, the Light Rune was a gift from the First Guardians to banish shadow. Its glow ties to Aria’s destiny, lighting her path against Umbraeth (Spire of Dawn Profile).

“It’s my dawn—cuts through the dark like a blade.”

Properties & Effects

The Light Rune casts a radiant burst, its azure glow piercing shadow and bolstering allies. Aria wields it to cleanse and shield in combat (Story Ch. 1).

Radiant Burst

Damages shadow foes within 5 meters.

Dawn Ward

Grants minor shadow resistance to allies.

Glow Mark

Illuminates dark areas for 30 seconds.

Origin

Inscribed by the First Guardians in the Spire of Dawn’s prime, the Light Rune was a bulwark against early shadows. Aria mastered it in Chapter 1, its glow flaring as she rose (Story Ch. 1).

Stats

Range: 5 meters

Duration: Instant (Glow: 30s)

Cooldown: 20 seconds

Magic Affinity: Light

Role in the Narrative

The Light Rune guides Aria through the Spire of Dawn, its radiance thwarting Umbraeth’s minions and lighting the guardians’ way. It’s key in shadow battles (Story Ch. 1-4).

Notable Wielders

  • Aria: “It’s my light—shines where shadows creep.”
  • First Guardians: “Carved it to hold the dark—eternal.”
  • Umbraeth: “That glow’s a fleeting spark—my shadow consumes.”

Legacy

From a guardian’s ward to Aria’s shining will, the Light Rune burns through darkness to victory. It fades triumphant as Umbraeth falls (Story Ch. 7).
