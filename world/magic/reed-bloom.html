Reed Bloom

The Healer’s Verdant Pulse

Appearance

The Reed Bloom is a pulse of green light, its glow sprouting tiny reed-like tendrils that shimmer and sway. Soft and alive, it blossoms in the Tribal Enclave’s embrace (Story Ch. 3, Mirra Profile).

Lore

Nurtured by the Tribal Enclave’s healers, the Reed Bloom was a gift of the earth to mend wounds. Its tendrils pulse with life, sustaining Mirra’s care for Aria’s crew (Tribal Enclave Profile).

“It’s my root—grows strength where it’s needed.”

Properties & Effects

The Reed Bloom radiates a healing aura, its green light restoring vitality over time. Mirra wields it to nurture in the Tribal Enclave (Story Ch. 3).

Life Pulse

Heals allies within 5 meters over 8 seconds.

Reed Shield

Grants minor damage resistance during heal.

Bloom Burst

Small heal spike at cast end.

Origin

Sprouted in the Tribal Enclave’s reed groves, the Reed Bloom was a healer’s balm for the broken. Mirra cultivated it in Chapter 3, its glow spreading as she joined Aria (Story Ch. 3).

Stats

Range: 5 meters

Duration: 8 seconds

Cooldown: 20 seconds

Magic Affinity: Nature

Role in the Narrative

The Reed Bloom sustains Mirra’s allies in the Tribal Enclave, its healing keeping Aria’s band strong against gruntlings. It’s key in group endurance (Story Ch. 3-4).

Notable Wielders

  • Mirra: “It’s my gift—mends us step by step.”
  • Tribal Healers: “Raised it to heal the weary—evergreen.”
  • Umbraeth: “That bloom won’t mend what I break.”

Legacy

From a healer’s touch to Mirra’s nurturing light, the Reed Bloom fosters hope through battle. It thrives as Umbraeth falls (Story Ch. 7).
