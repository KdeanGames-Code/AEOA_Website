Sand Veil

The Sandweaver’s Dusty Cloak

Appearance

The Sand Veil is a swirling cloud of golden dust, its grains shimmering as they rise in a blinding haze. Flowing like a desert storm, it cloaks the Sunken Sands in mystery (Story Ch. 3, Zara Profile).

Lore

Stirred by the Sunken Sands’ nomads, the Sand Veil was a shield against raiders, woven from the desert’s breath. Its haze hides Zara, guiding Aria through the dunes (Sunken Sands Profile).

“It’s my storm—blinds them while I weave ahead.”

Properties & Effects

The Sand Veil summons a dusty shroud, its grains obscuring vision and slowing foes. Zara uses it to evade and disorient in the Sunken Sands (Story Ch. 3).

Dust Cloud

Blinds enemies within 4 meters for 6 seconds.

Sand Drift

Slows enemy movement by 20% in range.

Veil Step

Boosts caster’s dodge chance briefly.

Origin

Raised from the Sunken Sands’ endless dunes, the Sand Veil was a nomad’s trick to vanish in the storm. Zara mastered it in Chapter 3, its dust swirling as she joined Aria (Story Ch. 3).

Stats

Range: 4 meters

Duration: 6 seconds

Cooldown: 18 seconds

Magic Affinity: Wind

Role in the Narrative

The Sand Veil cloaks Zara in the Sunken Sands, its haze thwarting foes and aiding Aria’s desert trek. It’s key in evasion and ambushes (Story Ch. 3-4).

Notable Wielders

  • Zara: “It’s my shroud—keeps me unseen in the sands.”
  • Sand Nomads: “Whipped it up to lose the hunters—elusive.”
  • Umbraeth: “That dust won’t blur my shadow’s reach.”

Legacy

From a nomad’s ruse to Zara’s desert dance, the Sand Veil obscures danger until triumph. It settles as Umbraeth fades (Story Ch. 7).
