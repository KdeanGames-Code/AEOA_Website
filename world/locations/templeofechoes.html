Temple of Echoes

A Cathedral Where Winds Sing

Overview

The Temple of Echoes stands in the central-east of the Valley of the Ancients, a wind-sung cathedral of towering mirrored spires that hum with ancient resonance. Its halls reflect both light and shadow, tainted by Umbraeth’s creeping corruption, a sacred enigma Aria must unravel.

Geography

Soaring spires of polished stone rise 15 meters, their mirrored surfaces catching the wind that whistles through the central-east plains. Shattered reflections line the interior halls, marred by Umbraeth’s black ichor, while gusts swirl dust across a stormy landscape dotted with cracked glyphs.

History

Built by the Ancients as a shrine to harness wind and sound, the Temple of Echoes fell silent when Umbraeth’s shadow cracked its mirrors. Elyra’s desperate chant here forged the Echo Chime, a relic lost within, awaiting Aria’s voice to reclaim it.

Inhabitants

Echo wraiths drift through the halls, their whispers carried by the wind, while mirror shards animate as reflective guardians. Umbraeth’s shadowed chanters haunt the spires, and Mira, the Echo Keeper, guards the temple, her voice a weapon against the silence.

Puzzles

The Mirror Chorus requires Aria to align three mirrored panels with wind bursts from the Gauntlet of Storms, reflecting a resonant beam to awaken the Echo Chime, all while dodging echo wraith shrieks that shatter focus.

Relics

The Echo Chime, a silver bell pulsing with wind song, rests in a mirrored vault, enhancing Aria’s abilities to disrupt Umbraeth’s forces. A Mirror Fragment, dropped by guardians, glows softly and boosts perception when held.

Companions

Mira, the Echo Keeper, emerges from the temple’s depths, her amber eyes gleaming beneath a flowing robe. Wielding a staff that amplifies sound, she aids Aria against chanters and wraiths, her chants guiding the way to the Echo Chime.

Role in the Narrative

The Temple of Echoes tests Aria’s harmony with the wind, the Echo Chime amplifying her fight against Umbraeth’s silence. Mira’s alliance unveils the Ancients’ sonic mastery, strengthening Aria’s resolve as she nears the Valley’s core.
