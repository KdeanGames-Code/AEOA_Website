The Wind-Sung Cathedral

The Temple of Echoes rises in the central-east Valley, a mirrored cathedral carved from obsidian, its spires resonating with haunting wind songs that weave through crystal chimes. Shadows flicker across its reflective surfaces, where raptor cries mingle with the whispers of the Ancients’ lost magic. Here, Aria steps into the temple’s labyrinthine halls, her Glyph-Blade echoing off glass walls as she deciphers the Wind Dial, Mirror Maze, and Echo Chamber—guided by Mira’s sharp ears, their journey a symphony of survival against Umbraeth’s shadow seeping through the cracks.
