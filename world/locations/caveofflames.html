The Ember Haven

The Cave of Flames yawns within the Living Jungle’s dense heart, a jagged maw of charred stone framed by blackened kapok stumps, their surfaces smoldering with faint embers that cast a flickering glow, the air thick with the sharp, acrid bite of ash and the musky scent of rot beneath crimson orchids. Ancient murals of the Ancients wielding fire against shadow glow faintly on damp walls, their ochre figures pulsing with latent magic, while raptor nests and insect swarms lurk in the shadows. Here, Aria ventures with Talyn, her Glyph-Blade igniting braziers to unlock the Ember Shard, each step a fiery duel against Umbraeth’s corruption, the cave’s heat a testament to the valley’s fiery past.
