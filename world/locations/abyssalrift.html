The Festering Wound

The Abyssal Rift yawns at the Valley’s heart, a festering chasm of black stone and writhing shadows, where Umbraeth’s corruption seeps like poison into the earth. A sickly green mist clings to its jagged edges, pierced by the screams of insects and the groans of corrupted golems. Here, Aria descends with Luna, the Shadow Seer, her Glyph-Blade cutting through darkness to uncover the Rift Core, Shadow Veil, and Corruption Crucible—each step a battle against Umbraeth’s warden, their bond tested in the rift’s depths as they seek to purge the valley’s curse.
