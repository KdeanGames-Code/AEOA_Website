The Jagged Ascent

The High Peaks rise along the Valley’s northern edge, jagged granite spires slashing through storm-laden skies, where howling winds carry the whispers of the Ancients’ final stand. Snow-dusted ledges cling to icy cliffs, shadowed by raptor nests and the eerie glow of Umbraeth’s corruption seeping through cracks. Here, Aria climbs with Zephyr, the Sky Warden, her Glyph-Blade slicing through gusts to uncover the Windlash Spire, Gale Gate, and Frost Vault—each step a test of endurance against casters and icy golems, their bond forged in the peaks’ relentless winds to confront Umbraeth’s final assault.
