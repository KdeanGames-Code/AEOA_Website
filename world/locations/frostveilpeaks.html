Frostveil Peaks

Frozen Heights Where Silence Reigns

Overview

The Frostveil Peaks tower over the northern edge of the Valley of the Ancients, a range of jagged, ice-clad summits shrouded in perpetual snowstorms. Ancient shrines pierce the frozen landscape, their secrets locked in ice and guarded by Umbraeth’s chilling corruption, awaiting Aria’s ascent.

Geography

Sheer cliffs of ice and stone rise 20 meters, flanked by glaciers that groan under their own weight. Frozen caves wind through the peaks, their walls etched with glowing glyphs, while Umbraeth’s black ichor stains the snow, seeping from fissures beneath a stormy, ash-grey sky.

History

The Frostveil Peaks once housed the Ancients’ sky temples, where they harnessed storms until Umbraeth’s betrayal froze their sanctuaries. Elyra’s final stand echoes here, her sacrifice sealing the Storm Crown deep within, a relic Aria must recover to defy the shadow.

Inhabitants

Frost wraiths glide through blizzards, their icy shrieks piercing the wind, while snowstalkers prowl with crystalline claws. Umbraeth’s frozen thralls guard the shrines, and Lyra, the Frost Seer, wanders the peaks, her visions aiding those who earn her trust.

Puzzles

The Ice Shard Lattice requires Aria to channel wind from the Gauntlet of Storms through three frozen prisms, melting a glyph-sealed gate with precise timing to avoid frost wraith ambushes, unlocking the Storm Crown’s chamber.

Relics

The Storm Crown, a silver circlet crackling with lightning, rests in an icy vault, enhancing Aria’s wind abilities against Umbraeth’s forces. A Frostfang Shard, dropped by snowstalkers, glows blue and boosts resilience when wielded.

Companions

Lyra, the Frost Seer, appears amidst the snow, her pale blue eyes glowing beneath a fur-lined hood. Armed with a staff of ice, she aids Aria against thralls and wraiths, her foresight guiding the path to the Storm Crown.

Role in the Narrative

The Frostveil Peaks test Aria’s endurance, the Storm Crown amplifying her power to shatter Umbraeth’s icy grip. Lyra’s visions reveal the Ancients’ storm mastery, forging a bond that drives Aria toward the Valley’s final confrontation.
