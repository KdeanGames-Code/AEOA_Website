Sunken Sands

Where dunes swallow the unwary

The Golden Abyss

The Sunken Sands stretch across the southwest Valley, a searing expanse of golden dunes rippling under a merciless sun, laced with quicksand pits and mirage-shrouded ruins. Scorpions skitter through the heat, while raptor shadows circle overhead, their cries echoing off ancient obelisks half-buried in sand. Here, Aria navigates the desert’s deadly embrace, her Glyph-Blade cutting through grit to uncover the Ancients’ secrets—Sandstone Compass, Quicksand Crucible, Dune Vault—guided by Kael’s keen eyes, their steps sinking into a crucible of endurance against Umbraeth’s creeping corruption.
