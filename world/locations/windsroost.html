The Lofty Perch

Wind’s Roost crowns the northern peaks, a lofty perch of granite and wind-carved stone, where gale-force winds howl against Umbraeth’s final assault. Icy outcrops shimmer under starlight, shadowed by raptor nests and the dark pulse of casters, as the Ancients’ last stand echoes through the cliffs. Here, Aria and Zephyr face the Warden of Shadows, her Glyph-Blade clashing with the Windspire Lock, Tempest Gate, and Starlight Vault—each gust a testament to their bond, purging Umbraeth’s corruption in a storm of light and wind to save the Valley.
