The Cliffside Refuge

The Tribal Enclave clings to the northeast Valley’s jagged cliffs, a network of stone platforms and thatched huts shadowed by Umbraeth’s creeping corruption. Torches flicker against the wind, illuminating totems carved with Ancients’ runes, while raptor cries echo from the heights. Here, Aria aligns with Sora, the Tribal Guardian, navigating the Cliff Totem, Shadow Bridge, and Ancestral Vault—her Glyph-Blade striking golems as they uncover the Reed Totem, forging a bond against the valley’s darkness in this precarious stronghold.
