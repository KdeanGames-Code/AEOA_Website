About the Game

Beneath the Teeth of the Sky, the Valley of the Ancients hums with forgotten magic and lurking dread. As Aria, the Last Guardian, wield relics and steel to banish Umbraeth—the shadow that consumed her mother Elyra’s light. A reactive world bends to your will; every choice echoes through time. Q4 2027.
