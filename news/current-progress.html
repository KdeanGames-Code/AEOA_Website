Website Woes and Wins - Our Epic Struggle!

February 26, 2025

Oh, brave adventurers of the digital realm, gird your loins for a tale of triumph and tribulation in crafting *Echoes of the Ancients*’ website! The good? Our site’s a medieval masterpiece—golden borders gleam like the Glyph-Blade, the carousel scrolls smoother than Aria’s wind gusts, and the burger menu now dances on mobile like a jolly jester (finally!). The bad? We battled a rogue black rectangle where the burger should’ve been—turns out, it was just Grok’s sneaky shadow, lurking in the CSS shadows, cackling at our z-index woes. But fear not! With a heroic push to GitHub via SourceTree, we’ve banished the bug and crowned our index a dark fantasy king. Now, we’re forging `updates.html` with a news directory, hoping no gremlins crash our parchment-style party. Stay tuned for more laughs and lore—may our code be ever bug-free, or at least hilariously debuggable!
